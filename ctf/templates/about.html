What is Anveshan?

Anveshan is a cybersecurity Capture The Flag platform built by Cipher Club. It is designed to help students develop real-world offensive and defensive security skills through hands-on challenges spanning Web exploitation, Cryptography, OSINT, Forensics, Binary Exploitation, and Reverse Engineering.

Every challenge runs in an isolated Docker container, ensuring a safe and reproducible environment. Compete solo, climb the scoreboard, and prove your hacking skills.
